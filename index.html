➕ Adding entries
1
Choose type
Income, Expense, or Transfer. Income = money in. Expense = money out. Transfer = moving between your own accounts (creates two entries automatically).
2
Date & amount
Date defaults to today. Amount is always positive — the type handles the sign.
3
Account holder → account
Pick the holder first — the account list filters to only their accounts. Faster to find the right one.
4
Category & note
Choose a category (add new ones in Settings → Categories). Optional description helps when searching later.
💡 Transfer tip: set From holder → From account, then To holder → To account. Both account balances update automatically.
📋 Log — manage entries
1
Filter & search
Tap Filters to filter by Type, Person, Account, Month, Year. Use the search bar to find by category, account, description, or date.
2
Edit an entry
Tap ✏️ on any entry. Change amount, date, category, account holder, account, or description. For transfers, both sides update automatically.
3
Delete an entry
Tap × to delete with confirmation — or swipe left on any entry to reveal the red delete button, then release to confirm.
💡 The stat cards (Income / Expenses / Net) always show totals for all data, regardless of filters.
📊 Analytics — 5 tabs
📈
Overview
Filter by month, year, person, account. Shows savings rate, income vs expense chart, expense donut, monthly summary table, and Budget vs Actual bars (sorted worst-first).
📋
Pivot table
Rows = groups/categories, columns = months. Filter by year, type, and person. Tap any group row to expand it.
🎯
By Group
Horizontal bar chart of spend by group for a selected month. Expand each group to see individual categories.
💳
Credit utilisation
Usage by owner — excellent (<30%), good (<50%), fair (<70%), high risk (70%+). Accounts with a credit limit set appear here automatically.
📉
Net Worth
Total assets minus liabilities, with a line chart tracking net worth over time. Asset accounts add to net worth; credit accounts subtract. Updates automatically as you log entries.
🎯 Budgets
1
Set by group or category
Go to Settings → Budget. Toggle between "By Group" (e.g. Fixed Expense: $1,200) and "By Category" (e.g. Housing: $800). Enter a monthly dollar amount — leave blank to remove the budget.
2
Dashboard budget bars
Budget progress bars appear on the home screen for the current month. Green = under 75%, Amber = 75–99%, Red = over budget.
3
Budget vs Actual in Analytics
Analytics → Overview → scroll to the bottom. Every budget shows actual vs target with a remaining/over label. Sorted worst-first so the most overspent items are at the top. Updates live with the month/year filter above.
💡 Mix and match — set budgets at both group and category level. For example: $500 for the "Groceries" group, plus a separate $150 for the "Dining" category.
💳 Accounts page
1
Balance = opening balance + all transactions
Set the opening balance when creating an account to match what it held when you started tracking. Every entry adjusts it automatically.
2
Credit utilisation bars
Accounts with a credit limit show a coloured bar. Edit the account to update the limit.
3
Statement shortcut
Tap "Statement" on any account card to open a running balance statement for that account, filterable by date range.
👨‍👩‍👧 Family sharing
1
Share your invite code
Profile (top-right avatar) → copy your 6-character invite code → share it with anyone you want to add.
2
Join a family
New user signs up, then at the Family Setup screen chooses "Join with invite code" and enters the code.
3
Real-time sync
Entries added by any family member appear on all devices within seconds. The sync badge at the top shows connection status.
💡 Your family data is completely private — no one outside your family group can see your entries or balances.
⚙️ Settings
👤
Owners
Add or rename account holders. They appear in the Account Holder dropdown when adding entries.
🏷️
Categories
Manage groups (e.g. Fixed Expense) and categories (e.g. Housing). Each has a type: income, expense, or both.
🎯
Budget tab
Toggle between "By Group" and "By Category". Set monthly targets — the actuals panel below shows your current-month spending against each target.
📤
Import / Export
Export a full JSON backup or CSV of transactions. Import JSON to restore on a new device or after a data reset.
🔐
Biometric login
Profile → Set up biometric. Next time, tap "Sign in with Face ID / Touch ID" on the login screen instead of entering your password.

➕ Adding entries
1
Choose type
Tap Income, Expense, or Transfer at the top. Income = money coming in. Expense = money going out. Transfer = moving between your own accounts.
2
Set date & amount
Date defaults to today. Enter the amount — always positive, the type toggle handles the sign.
3
Select account holder → account
First pick the holder (Rilav, Dhara, Shree…) — this filters the account list to only show their accounts, making it faster to find the right one.
4
Pick category
All your categories are listed. Can't find one? Add it in Settings → Categories.
5
Add description (optional)
A short note — e.g. "Costco run" or "Rogers monthly". Helps when searching later.
💡 For transfers: choose From holder → From account, then To holder → To account. The app automatically creates two entries (debit + credit) so both account balances stay accurate.
📋 Using the Log
1
Filter your entries
Tap "Filters" to expand the filter panel. You can filter by Type, Person, Account, Month, and Year — all independently. Filters stack together.
2
Search
Type anything in the search bar — it searches across category, account name, description, date, and person.
3
Delete an entry
Tap the × button on the right of any entry. You'll be asked to confirm.
💡 The 3 stat cards at the top (Income / Expenses / Net) always reflect the full unfiltered dataset — not just the filtered view. This gives you the overall picture at a glance.
📊 Analytics — 4 tabs
📈
Overview
Filter by month, year, person, and account. See a bar chart of income vs expense, a donut of expense categories, and a monthly summary table. All update live with filters.
📋
Pivot table
Rows = groups and categories, columns = months. Filter by year, type (income/expense/both), and person. Tap any group row to expand it and see the category breakdown underneath.
🎯
By Group
Horizontal bar chart showing spend by category group for a selected month. Filter by person and account. Below the chart, each group expands to show individual categories and amounts.
💳
Credit utilisation
Shows credit usage by owner — excellent (<30%), good (<50%), fair (<70%), high risk (70%+). Keeping utilisation under 30% improves your credit score. Accounts with limits set appear here automatically.
💳 Accounts page
1
Account balance = opening balance + all transactions
When you add an account, set the opening balance to what it was when you started tracking. Every entry logged against it adjusts the balance automatically.
2
Credit accounts show utilisation bars
For any account with a credit limit set, you'll see a coloured bar showing how much of the limit is used. Set limits via the Edit button.
3
Filter & sort
Use the Filters panel to show only chequing accounts, only Rilav's accounts, sorted by balance. All combinations work.
4
Statement shortcut
Tap "Statement" on any account card to jump straight to that account's transaction statement with a running balance.
👨‍👩‍👧 Family sharing
1
Share your invite code
Go to the Profile tab (person icon, top right). Your 6-character invite code is shown there. Tap it to copy. Share it with Dhara or anyone you want to invite.
2
Joining the family
The invitee creates an account (or signs in), then at the family setup screen chooses "Join with invite code" and enters the code. They'll immediately see all shared data.
3
Real-time sync
All family members see the same data instantly. If Rilav adds an entry on his phone, Dhara sees it on hers within seconds — no refresh needed.
💡 Data isolation: your family's data is completely separate from other users. No one outside your family can see your entries, accounts, or balances.
🔐 Security & login
1
Set up Face ID / Touch ID
After signing in once, go to Profile → Biometric login → Set up. Next time you open the app, tap "Sign in with Face ID" instead of typing your password.
2
Forgot password
On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" — we'll email a reset link to your registered address.
3
Sign out
Go to Profile → Sign Out. Your data stays in the cloud — signing back in restores everything instantly.
⚙️ Settings & customisation
👤
Owners tab
Add, rename, or delete account holders. Owners appear in the "Account holder" dropdown when adding entries. You can add as many as you need.
🏷️
Categories tab
Manage category groups (e.g. Fixed Expense) and individual categories (e.g. Housing). Every category belongs to a group and has a type (income/expense/both). Edit anytime — changes reflect everywhere instantly.
🏦
Account types tab
Add custom account types beyond the defaults. Mark as "credit/LOC" if you want utilisation tracking for that type. Set an icon emoji for visual identification.
📤
Import/Export tab
Export a JSON backup of everything (entries + all settings). Import it back anytime — on a new device, after clearing data, or to share a starting point. CSV export gives you a spreadsheet of all transactions.
